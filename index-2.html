Barcelona

Baercelona is acity on the coast of norteastern Spain. It is the capital and largest city of Spain, and it is home to around 4.8 million people. It is one of the largest metropolises on the Mediterranean sea, located on the coast between the mouthes of the rivers Llobregat and Besos, and bounded by the west by the Serra de Collserola mountain range, the tallest peak of which is 512metres (1,680 feet) high

Barcelona has a rich cultural heritage and it is today an important cultural centre and a major tourist destination
Moscow

Russia's prestigious capital holds its own on global stage, with stunning architecture, museums and a variety of cultural offerings making it an enticing place to visit any time of year. Set foot on the black cobblestones of Moscow's ionic Red Square and you'll be treated to a breathtakingpanorama dominated by the Kremlin's extensive brick walls and St. Basil's Cathedral

You can also catch some world-class ballet and opera at the Bolshoi, a six-tier auditorium which has hosted performance since 1825
